Description

Use case

Buying the dips is the age old strategy of the stock market. But most of the times we are not sure how much and when to reinvest in existing stock. This calculator will help you to find how much average investment your stock is going to have after reinvestment. It will help you to decide that after how much fall you should decide to reinvest in the stock.

How to use:

  • Calculator will ask you total of four values.
  • In the first buy give two values.
  • Number of existing units or quantity and current cost or current average cost.
  • After that give values of your second buy or the purchase you want to make.
  • In that also give number of units you are willing to purchase and cost of the single unit.
  • After clicking on Calculate it will give you the average cost and total number of shares.
