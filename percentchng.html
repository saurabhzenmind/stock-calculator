Description

Use Case

Some times you want to check that how much percentage stock was fall or raised from certain point. And if it fall or raised to certain point how much it will affect my investment, percentage wise. In this case you can use this calculator to find percent change of two points.

How To Use:

  • Calculator will ask you two values.
  • In first box give value of Initial point, the point from which you want know how much percantage second point differ.
  • In the second box give the value of second point.
  • After clicking on calculate you will get the percent change from first point to second point.
