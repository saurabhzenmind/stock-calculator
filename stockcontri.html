Description

Use case

Most of the portfolios consists of more than 2 stocks. And each stock have different amount of total investment. In that case when we want to check total contribution of each stock in our portfolio then at that time we can use this calculator to check how much percentage each stock occupied in our portfolio. When we want to allocate percent money sector wise, at that time we can use this calculator.

How to use:

  • Calculator asks you two values.
  • First you type amount of your total investment.
  • Secondly type amount of that stock of which you want to know its percentage.
  • After clicking on calculate you will get total percentage of that particular stock in your portfolio.
